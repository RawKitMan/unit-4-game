Crystal Collector

Welcome to Crystal Collector, a simple game with simple rules.

How to play


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.You have four crystals below. Each crystal is worth a random number of points. The amount of points given are unknown until you click on them. Your goal is to get an amount of points equal to the random number. If you go over, you will lose. Whether you win or lose, a new game will start with a new random number and the crystals will change their value.
